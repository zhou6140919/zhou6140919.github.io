# Introduction

SpaCy model [zh_core_web_trf](https://spacy.io/models/zh#zh_core_web_trf) is trained on OntoNotes 5 by bert-base-chinese. The accuracy of part-of-speech tags is 0.92, which seems to be less effective than [HanLP models](https://github.com/hankcs/HanLP/tree/master) trained on CTB8 (whose accuracy is 0.967).
**But it needs to be proved that HanLP indeed has higher accuracy and is trained faster than SpaCy on same datasets.**
